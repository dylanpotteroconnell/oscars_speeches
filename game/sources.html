About This Game

This trivia game focuses on 8 major categories: Best Picture, Directing, the four acting awards, and the two screenplay awards. Speeches are filtered to those from 1993 onward for better recognizability.

The speech snippets, redactions, and hints were processed with the help of AI to select interesting excerpts and hide identifying information.
